Logo
The Sprout logo system includes three variants — a logotype, landscape lockup, and icon mark. Each serves a different context and scale.

Logo Variants

Use the variant best suited for the available space and context. The logotype is the primary wordmark; the lockup adds the character for brand moments; the icon is for app icons and compact surfaces.

Logotype

Wordmark + tagline. Primary logo for headers, navigation, and product UI.

Landscape Lockup

Character + wordmark + tagline. For marketing, splash screens, and brand moments.

Icon

App icon. For favicons, app stores, and compact surfaces.

Logotype Colors

The logotype comes in three color variants: brand (green), black, and white. Use the variant that provides the best contrast against its background.

Brand
Black
White

App Icons

Two icon variants exist: Primary (kid experience) and Parent (parent oversight). The parent variant includes a shield badge in the corner.

Primary
Main app icon for the kid experience.
Parent
Parent app icon with shield badge for oversight features.

Clear Space

Maintain clear space around the logo equal to at least half the height of the mark on all sides. This ensures the logo breathes and is never crowded by adjacent elements.

0.5x 0.5x 0.5x 0.5x

Minimum Size

Each variant has a minimum size below which details are lost and the mark becomes illegible.

Logotype Min 80px wide
Lockup Min 120px wide
Icon Min 32px

Usage Rules

Follow these guidelines whenever placing the Sprout logo in product, marketing, or partner contexts.

✓Always scale the logo uniformly — never stretch or distort
✓Use the logotype for product UI (headers, nav, settings)
✓Use the landscape lockup for marketing, onboarding, and splash screens
✓Use the icon mark for favicons, app icons, and compact surfaces
✓Reference SVG assets by path (<img src="/brand/assets/...">) — never inline SVG markup
✗Don't place the logo on busy backgrounds without sufficient contrast
✗Don't recolor the logo — use only the defined brand palette
✗Don't add effects (drop shadows, gradients, outlines) to the logo
✗Don't rearrange the lockup — the character, wordmark, and tagline positions are fixed
