Guidelines

Guiding principles for motion in Sprout.

1

Transitions must be functional

Every transition should serve a purpose — guide attention, confirm an action, or show state change. Avoid decorative motion that adds no information.

2

Respect reduced motion

Wrap non-essential transitions in @media (prefers-reduced-motion: no-preference). Essential state indicators (spinners, progress) should remain but may reduce intensity.

3

Active state pattern

Pressed/active states always use box-shadow: none; transform: translateY(Npx) where N matches the resting shadow offset. This creates a physical “push” feel.

4

Enter/exit asymmetry

Elements enter with ease-out (fast start, gentle land) and exit with ease-in (gentle start, fast departure). Exits are slightly shorter than enters — e.g., sheet enter 300ms vs. exit 250ms.
