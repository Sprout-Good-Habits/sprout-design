Nudge Sheet
A bottom sheet that invites the user to send a quick reaction to someone’s achievement. Composes Conversational Feedback, emoji Tiles, a Button, and a Sheet. All transitions follow iOS motion principles.

Prototype

Tap an emoji tile to select it. The sheet enters with a spring animation; tile selection uses a spring pop.

The nudge sheet is a composition of design system components inside a standard Sheet.

Reduced motion

  • When prefers-reduced-motion: reduce is active, all spring and slide transitions are disabled. The sheet appears immediately and tile selection is instant.

Reaction vocabulary

All reactions are positive — no shame, no negative feedback. Each emoji maps to a specific dimension of encouragement.

EmojiDimensionSubtitle text
🔥ConsistencyYou’re on fire! Keep up that streak
♥️SupportLoved this effort! You showed up today.
👏EffortNice job! You got it done.
⭐️MasteryLook at you! You leveled up!
  • ✓ Every reaction celebrates something the child did well.
  • ✓ The subtitle updates to match the selected emoji, reinforcing the specific dimension.
  • ✗ Never include negative, shaming, or corrective language in reactions.

Behavior

  • The sheet appears over the sky-blue background with a scrim overlay.
  • Exactly one emoji tile is always selected (single-select). The first tile is selected by default.
  • Tapping a tile selects it with a spring pop animation and deselects the previous one.
  • The subtitle text and leading icon update to match the selected reaction.
  • The primary button sends the selected reaction and dismisses the sheet.
  • Swiping down on the handle or tapping the scrim dismisses the sheet.

Accessibility

  • The sheet is a modal with role="dialog" and aria-modal="true".
  • Focus is trapped within the sheet while open.
  • Emoji tiles use role="radiogroup" with individual tiles as role="radio" and aria-checked.
  • The selected tile should have aria-checked="true"; others aria-checked="false".
  • Arrow keys navigate between tiles; Space/Enter selects.
