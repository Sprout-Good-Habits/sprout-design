Spacing

Padding and gaps in parent chat fall into five composition roles. Pick the role first, then the token. The full token scale lives at kid-design-system / spacing; this page narrows it to chat usage.

Inside a card: 12 or 16

Two options for padding inside a card surface. Pick by content density.

12px (tight)
Use for high-density
Banner content, message bubble (vertical), action button (vertical). When the surface contains a single line or is itself small.
Hi there
--spacing-lg
16px (default)
Use for everything else
Composer pill, confirm-card, sheet body, artifact card. The default. Reach for this unless you have a reason not to.
Hi there
--spacing-xl

For bubbles specifically: 12 vertical, 16 horizontal. The asymmetry keeps the bubble compact while leaving readable side margins for the text.

Sheet bottom padding

Safe area rule

Every bottom sheet needs at least --spacing-4xl (32px) at the bottom to clear the iOS home indicator. Use --spacing-5xl (40px) when the last child is interactive (a button, a list item) so taps don't land on the indicator.

Top padding inside a sheet is variable: reasoning-transcript uses 0 (header sits flush at the top), artifact-sheet uses 0, context-menu uses 16. The bottom is the constant, not the top.

What about gap?

The same tokens are used for gap as for padding. The five roles map cleanly:

  • Tight stack (2 to 4): gap inside a typographic stack (title above subtitle).
  • Inline row (6 to 8): gap between an icon and its label, or between two icon buttons.
  • Inside a card (12 to 16): gap between rows in a card (confirm-card rows, sheet header to body).

Sheets use 16 (--spacing-xl) as their internal gap between header, body, and footer. Composer pill stacks its children at 16 too.

Open questions
  • 56px hardcoded alignment on .msg-sender and .msg-actions (padding-left: 56px) is not magic: it's 32px avatar + 8px gap + 16px bubble inset = 56. Document it as derived, not as a token.
  • 10px gaps on chat-banner (spinner to text) and reply-pill don't fit the scale. Likely should snap to 8 (--spacing-md) or 12 (--spacing-lg).
  • 7px feature item padding in the parent-chat-3 page-level styles. Should snap to 6 (--spacing-sm) or 8 (--spacing-md).
  • Layout offsets (62 / 128 / 160) are scaffolding, not spacing. They depend on status bar + header heights. Keep them out of the spacing scale.
  • Composer area bottom (bottom: 20px) uses --spacing-2xl in some places and a raw 20px in others. Worth aligning.
