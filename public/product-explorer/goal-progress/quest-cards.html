Quest Cards
Every Quest card variant from the spec — 6 lifecycle states across 4 tiers. Hover any cell to enlarge.
Quest cards are the smallest unit of progress on the home screen. They render differently per tier (carousel, list row, compact tile, village micro-card) but share the same 6 states. Tier 1‑2 and Tier 3 are reused from Leti's existing /kid-home/_quest-card-states.html shape so they stay 1:1 with the live design system.
