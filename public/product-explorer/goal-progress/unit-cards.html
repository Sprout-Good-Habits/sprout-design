Goal Progress System
Unit Cards
Unit cards live inside the Goal Lobby. Each unit is one step toward a larger goal. Seven status states across two tiers, plus a connected sequence preview.
Unit cards are the atoms inside a Goal Lobby. Where Quest cards live on the home screen, Unit cards live in the lobby's chosen layout (Sequential, Gallery, Checklist…). They share lifecycle states with quest cards but add two extras: To do (queued, distinct from Not started) and Upcoming (locked behind earlier units). Stars indicate the kid's grade for that unit.
