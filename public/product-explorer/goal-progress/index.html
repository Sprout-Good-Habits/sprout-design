Goal Progress System
A focused fork for exploring how a kid moves through a goal — from lobby to unit to agent interaction to verification — at two age tiers.

What this is. The Goal Lobby is the surface that holds goal progress. Inside it, units have a richer lifecycle than just done/not done: a quest can finish deterministically (a quiz), or be auto-approved by the AI after a moment of thinking, or get escalated to a parent for human review. This explorer lets you click through that loop on real device frames.

Two playgrounds, one loop. Tier 2 (younger, big visual) and Tier 3 (older, denser list) show the same end-to-end click-through: home → lobby → pick a unit → run the quest → see the unit's state update in the lobby.
