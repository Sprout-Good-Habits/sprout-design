The Trade-Off Engine
Instant Gratification
🎮

10 💎

VS
Delayed Gratification
🌌
Space Atlas Book
150 💎

This felt trade-off IS the financial literacy. Every gem spent on screen time is a gem not saved toward something bigger. Kids learn opportunity cost by living it, not by being told about it.
